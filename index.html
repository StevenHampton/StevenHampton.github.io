About me as a person

I have always enjoyed technology and tech in my life, but have always pursued other paths in life. I Spent some time in the Army, and deployed twice to Iraq, shaping me as a person. After i got out of military I decided to go to college but quickly found that I did not enjoy this path, so I took time and worked in varying careers such as a corrections officer and an electrician. I found that i didn't feel intellectually stimulated in these fields and decided to purse my passion for technology. Not wanting to go back to college at this point in my life I began to research different tech schools and programs. I found one in San Antonio by the name of Codeup, that teaches a person to become a full stack developer in an intense 4 month program. From day one this class ignited my passion and I have felt more excited than I have in a long time just writing programs. I love the ability to take an idea of something, come up with a game plan to make it happen then to slowly create it. I find it so exciting whenever a page or program loads the exact way you want it to. I look forward to continuing a long career in tech and even now feel like I would enjoy furthering my education.
